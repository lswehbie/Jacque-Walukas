Food Fellas









About



King establishes a melt-in-your mouth name for himself by venturing into the fountain of wealth in the fast-food industry.
He is seduced by money, sex, power, and fast food by the slick talk of his fast-food mogul counterpart, Ron the Don, who’s slimy ethics leads them all to bloody demise.
