About



Yacov Ronen is asked by Rabbi Malamud, a leader in the contemporary Brooklyn NYC Hasidic community Yacov has previously left, to take a shift watching over the body of a deceased Holocaust survivor, Mr. Litvak. The body, Yacov Ronen, and an unexpected demon named the Mazzik remain at home together until dawn.
